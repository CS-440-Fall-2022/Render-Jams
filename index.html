Render Jam is a showcase for the rendering abilities gained through the course CS 440: Computer Graphics, at Habib University. Students will aim to display their creativity and graphics skills, to be displayed in front of the community. Many renders feature interactivity, and instructions are given alongside each render, along with a description of the scene. Take your time to appreciate the effort that went into each of these renders, and then place your votes on your favourite renders. The winning render will be featured in the syllabus of the Fall 2023 offering for CS 440!
